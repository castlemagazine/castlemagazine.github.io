WHAT IS CASTLE?

Castle is a modern magazine about video games.

We want to underscore the importance of gaming in our cultural landscape through themed issues that investigate the worlds they inhabit and affect.

WHAT'S THE FIRST ISSUE ABOUT?

Our first issue will explore the intersection between video games and health. We want to chart the changing purpose of health as a game mechanic, and go beyond games to explore how they affect our minds and bodies.
